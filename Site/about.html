About Us

The Newman Law Group is dedicated to providing our clients with the highest caliber of service.

Our attorneys practiced law at large law firms and we use the professionalism learned there together with our years of legal and business experience to provide top notch service at a reasonable cost. We believe strongly that every client is entitled to attentive and thorough service and a through explanation of the legal process.

We pride ourselves on our creative problem solving based upon practical knowledge and the highest caliber training. We provide legal services to clients ranging from individuals to start-up businesses and ongoing companies.
